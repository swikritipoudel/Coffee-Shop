Coffee Trivia

When did coffee first appear in the United Stated?

According to Jennifer Lewis, a freelancer writer who specializes in culture, Coffee was introduced to America in the early 17th century through Jamestown, Virginia. Despite early popularity, tea remained the preferred drink until the Boston Tea Party fueled a patriotic shift towards coffee consumption in 1773. (Source: COFFEE OR DIE MAGAZINE)
